iibsasho Marketplace Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Last Updated: August 3, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to iibsasho, a marketplace platform that connects buyers and sellers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website.

Key Points:
  • We collect information to provide and improve our marketplace services
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data
  • We never sell your personal information to third parties
  • You have control over your data and privacy settings

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Profile Data: Name, email address, phone number, profile picture
  • Authentication: Login credentials and verification status
  • Preferences: Language, notification settings, category interests

2.2 Listing Information

  • Product Details: Title, description, price, category, condition
  • Images and Media: Photos and videos of items for sale
  • Location Data: General location for pickup/delivery (city/region level)

2.3 Communication Data

  • Messages: Chat conversations between buyers and sellers
  • Support Tickets: Customer service communications
  • Ratings and Reviews: Feedback and ratings from transactions

2.4 Technical Information

  • Device Data: Device type, operating system, browser information
  • Usage Analytics: App usage patterns, feature usage, performance metrics
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, error logs

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Provision

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Processing and displaying your listings
  • Facilitating communication between buyers and sellers
  • Processing payments and transactions
  • Providing customer support

3.2 Safety and Security

  • Verifying user identity and preventing fraud
  • Monitoring for suspicious or prohibited activities
  • Enforcing our Terms of Service and community guidelines
  • Protecting against spam and abuse

3.3 Communication

  • Sending transaction-related notifications
  • Providing updates about your listings and account
  • Delivering customer support responses
  • Sending promotional communications (with your consent)

3.4 Improvement and Analytics

  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve our services
  • Testing new features and functionality
  • Generating aggregate statistics and reports
  • Personalizing your app experience

4. Information Sharing

4.1 With Other Users

When you create a listing or engage in transactions, certain information is shared:

  • Your public profile information (name, profile picture, ratings)
  • Listing details and images
  • Chat messages related to transactions
  • General location (city/region level only)

4.2 With Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our platform:

  • Cloud Infrastructure: Supabase, Firebase (data hosting and processing)
  • Payment Processing: Secure payment gateways
  • Analytics: App performance and usage analytics
  • Push Notifications: OneSignal for app notifications
  • Email Services: Transactional email delivery

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes and government requests
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Prevent fraud or illegal activities
  • Enforce our Terms of Service

5. Data Security

5.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Data Minimization: We collect only necessary information

5.2 Your Security Responsibilities

  • Keep your login credentials secure and confidential
  • Report suspicious activities or security concerns immediately
  • Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication when available
  • Keep your app updated to the latest version

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 Access and Control

  • Account Settings: Update your profile and preferences
  • Data Export: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Data Deletion: Delete your account and associated data
  • Communication Preferences: Opt-out of promotional communications

6.2 Regional Rights

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under local privacy laws:

  • GDPR (EU): Right to rectification, erasure, portability, and objection
  • CCPA (California): Right to know, delete, and opt-out of data sales
  • Other Jurisdictions: Rights as provided by applicable local laws

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Inactive Accounts: Data may be retained for up to 2 years after last activity
  • Legal Requirements: Some data retained longer for legal compliance
  • Account Deletion: Most data deleted within 30 days of account closure

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses for EU data transfers
  • Adequacy decisions where applicable
  • Other appropriate transfer mechanisms as required by law

10. Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or integrate with third-party services. This Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party services. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any third-party services you use.

Third-Party Services We Use:

  • Supabase: Database and backend services
  • Firebase: Authentication and analytics
  • OneSignal: Push notification services
  • Google Maps: Location services
  • Payment Processors: Transaction processing

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes:

  • We will post the updated policy on this page
  • We will update the "Last Updated" date
  • For significant changes, we will notify you via email or app notification
  • Continued use of our service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy
